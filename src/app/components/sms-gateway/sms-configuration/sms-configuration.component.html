Dlt process steps

As per Telecom Regulatory TRAI guidelines, for sending SMS, each SMS has to be registered with DTL platform. We have been observing that some of our clients are not able to send SMS. To facilitate extended service to Proctur’s clients, We have come up with option 1 as an extended process.

Option 1 -For immediate sending SMS, clients can use Sender Id as “PRCTUR”, but in this case brand name will be Proctur, for each SMS.

• The option 1 is valid for a limited period only till your SMS Content Templates separately approved from DLT.

• This is New process introduced by TRAI. We are also making efforts to smoothen the process from Proctur end .

Option 2- Institute who are looking for SMS delivery with their own brand name. Please download the document. https://bit.ly/2RjEcUs and follow the process.
