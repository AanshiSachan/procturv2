Dear Sir/Madam,
As per Telecom Regulatory TRAI guidelines, for sending SMS, each SMS has to be register with DLT platform. SMS will only be delivered if that SMS Content Template is approved on DLT. Kindly ensure to apply the SMS Content Templates separately for each kind of SMS that you are sending. Steps to be followed.
(A) Entity Registration Process
1) For faster response, it’s requested for register on MTNL. Entity Registration Process for MTNL Signup Link:https://www.ucc-mtnl.in/.
2) Form Filling: Fill the form and upload the required documents.
3) Form Submission: Submit the application and you will get the approval.
4) Headers Registration: Once registration is approved, kindly login and add your Headers (Sender ID) and it will be approved in a day or two.
5) Test SMS: Once Header is approved on DLT platform, it will be synced on Global Platform in around 24 hrs and you will then be able to use your Header for sending SMS.
(B) Get approval for your SMS content on DLT platform.
Make a list of SMS content that expected to Send through Proctur and get it approved on DLT. 1) Find the link of Proctur system generated SMS, which is expected to approved from DLT. (Download the template-“ https://bit.ly/3sDxMgx ”).
2) And For custom SMS, needs to upload individually on DLT form, and get it approved.
3) As it gets approved follow the refer (C) section.
(C) SMS system Login.
1) An Individual needs to contact to Proctur support team using help section from application. Make sure SMS templates are approved at DLT end.
2) Proctur Team will share sub-account of Pinnacle SMS service provider login details, where approved SMS from DLT to be get uploaded.
3) Add custom approved SMS and send it from Proctur.
